Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

What kind of photos should I upload?
Upload 5–10 casual selfies with good natural lighting. Include a mix of angles — front-facing, slight profile, smiling and neutral expressions. Avoid heavy makeup, filters, or sunglasses. The more variety, the better your results.
How long does it take to get my headshots?
Very fast — most headshots are ready in under a minute. You'll receive a notification as soon as they're done. Complex styles may take slightly longer, but typically it's less than 60 seconds.
Are my photos private and secure?
Absolutely. Your uploaded photos are encrypted during transfer and processing. We never use your photos to train our AI models, and we never share them with third parties.
What if I don't like my headshots?
We offer a 7-day free refund. If you're not satisfied with your headshots, contact our support team and we'll issue a full refund — no questions asked. We're confident you'll love the results.
Can I use the headshots commercially?
Yes! You receive full commercial rights to all generated headshots. Use them on LinkedIn, your company website, email signatures, business cards, speaker profiles — anywhere you need a professional presence.
Can I use AI headshots on LinkedIn?
Absolutely! AI-generated headshots are perfect for LinkedIn. They look professional, natural, and indistinguishable from traditional photography. Thousands of professionals use them for their LinkedIn profile, and they're fully compliant with LinkedIn's photo guidelines.
How is this different from a traditional photographer?
You save 90%+ on cost, skip the scheduling and studio commute, and get dozens of variations instead of just a handful. For a fraction of the price of one professional session, you get hundreds of headshots with different styles and backdrops.
